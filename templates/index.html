Prevention Protection & Care

Amidst the COVID-19 crisis situation, we should understand our duty in taking care of others and our family. Due to the ongoing pandemic, many people are getting affected. One of the main reasons for this exponential increase in the number of COVID-19 cases is the lack of awareness. In this pandemic situation, wearing a face mask should be an integral part of our lives. People are not taking this step seriously, due to which according to the recent statistical report, most of the spread of Coronavirus disease occurs due to aerial transmission. It is said in a scientific report that we can prevent COVID-19 90% transmission by following proper safety and precautionary measures such as wearing mask in public areas, regular temperature monitoring, automated and contactless parking system and sanitizers delivery. College Campus and offices are also facing lot of problems in checking wherether eveyone is following the specified COVID-19 protocols. In this project we attempt to automate and supervice the most important protocols of COVID-19. We have created this Web API for Face Mask Detection, Automated Face based Attendence System, Automatic Number Plate Recognition, Regular Temperature Monitoring, Handsfree Sanitizer Despenser and Smart Parking System.
